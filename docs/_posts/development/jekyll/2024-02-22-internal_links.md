Syntax to link to internal posts:
````markdown
[ShownName]({% post_url 2024-02-22-jekyll_headers %})
````
This will result in:
[ShownName]({% post_url 2024-02-22-jekyll_headers %})

Lookup further information on the official [docs (https://jekyllrb.com/docs/)](https://jekyllrb.com/docs/)
